Recipe for Pizza

This recipe is great for beginners and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

    Indegredients
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • Bread flour
    Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 230C
  2. Place warm water in a bowl, add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand for 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour,oil and salt to the mixuter
  4. Tourn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and roll.
  5. Spread crust with sauce and toppings
  6. Bake in the oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
